1. Information we collect

We collect information in a few ways: what you give us, what our service generates, and what our tools observe.

What you give us

When you contact Annsa — by email, phone call, or our contact form — you may provide:

  • Your name and the name of your dental practice
  • Your email address and phone number
  • Information about your practice: size, location, front desk setup, call volume
  • Any other information you choose to share during our conversation

What our service generates

When you use Annsa's voice receptionist service, the following is generated:

  • Call metadata: date, time, duration, and outcome of each call
  • Appointment booking records created during calls
  • Escalation emails sent to you from the AI receptionist
  • Monthly proof reports showing calls answered and appointments booked

Important: Annsa's voice AI handles calls in real time. We configure our systems to not store the full audio content or transcripts of patient calls for your clients. Call recordings and transcripts are disabled by default on our client assistants. This reduces PHI storage risk for dental practices using our service.

What our tools observe

When you visit our website, we collect:

  • Browser type and version
  • Pages visited and time spent on them
  • Referring website or source
  • General geographic location (country and city level, not precise)

2. How we use your information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Deliver our voice receptionist service to your dental practice
  • Send you payment confirmations, onboarding instructions, and monthly proof reports
  • Respond to your questions and support requests
  • Process subscription payments and cancellations via Lemon Squeezy
  • Notify you of service updates, billing changes, or important notices
  • Understand how visitors use our website so we can improve it
  • Detect and prevent fraud or abuse of our service

We do not use your information for advertising. We do not sell or rent your personal information to anyone.

3. How we share your information

We share your information only in these specific situations:

Service providers

We use third-party services to operate Annsa. These providers only receive the information they need to do their job, and are contractually required to protect it:

  • VAPI (voice AI platform) — handles call routing, AI responses, and phone number provisioning. Their privacy policy applies to data processed during calls.
  • Lemon Squeezy (payment processing) — processes your subscription payments. They handle payment card data directly; we never see your full card number.
  • n8n (automation) — our automation platform sends you emails and notifications based on your account activity.
  • Gmail (email delivery) — used to send you service-related emails.
  • Telegram (notifications) — sends us alerts when you make a payment or when the AI needs to escalate something to us.

Legal requirements

We may disclose your information if required by law — for example, in response to a valid court order, government request, or to protect our legal rights.

Business transfers

If Annsa is ever acquired by another company or merges with one, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction. We'll notify you before that happens.

With your permission

We will never share your information for any other purpose without your explicit permission.

4. Data retention

We keep your information for as long as you have an active subscription:

  • Active subscriptions: We retain your account information, call metadata, and booking records while your subscription is active.
  • After cancellation: We delete your account data within 30 days of cancellation. Payment records are retained longer as required by financial regulations.
  • Website visitor data: Analytics data is retained for 26 months before being anonymized or deleted.

You can request deletion of your data at any time by emailing hello@annsa.ai.

5. Your rights

You have the following rights over your personal information:

  • Access: You can request a copy of all personal information we hold about you.
  • Correction: If any information we hold is incorrect, you can ask us to fix it.
  • Deletion: You can request that we delete your personal information. We will do so within 30 days.
  • Objection: You can ask us to stop using your information for certain purposes.
  • Portability: You can request your data in a commonly used, machine-readable format.

To exercise any of these rights, email hello@annsa.ai. We'll respond within one business day during US business hours.

6. California residents — Your CCPA rights

If you are a California resident, you have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA):

  • Right to know: You can request disclosure of what personal information we collect, use, disclose, and sell about you.
  • Right to delete: You can request deletion of your personal information.
  • Right to opt out: You can opt out of the sale of your personal information. We do not sell personal information.
  • Right to non-discrimination: We will not discriminate against you for exercising any of your CCPA rights.

To submit a verified California privacy request, email hello@annsa.ai with the subject line "CCPA Request."

7. AI disclosure for California and Texas residents

California residents: Under California Assembly Bill 3030 (effective January 2025), businesses must disclose when they use generative artificial intelligence to interact with consumers. Annsa's voice AI uses generative AI to handle calls for dental practices. This disclosure is included in the setup documentation we provide to each client.

Texas residents: Under Texas Senate Bill 1188 (effective September 2025), similar disclosure requirements apply. Our service documentation includes the required AI disclosure notice for use in your practice.

For your patients: Each dental practice using Annsa is responsible for informing their patients that the phone receptionist may use AI. We provide the exact disclosure language in the client onboarding materials.

8. Data security

We take the following steps to protect your information:

  • All data transmitted to and from our service is encrypted using TLS/SSL
  • Access to customer data is restricted to authorized personnel only
  • Payment card data is handled directly by Lemon Squeezy — we never store your full card number
  • Our automation platform (n8n) is configured to use secure, authenticated connections
  • Passwords and API keys are stored securely and never shared in plain text

No method of data transmission over the internet is 100% secure. While we take reasonable measures to protect your data, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

If you continue using Annsa's services after changes take effect, you accept the updated policy.
