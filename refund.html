1. How to cancel

Cancel anytime — no notice period required, no cancellation fee.

To cancel, email us at ammarthemediamanager@gmail.com with the subject line "Cancel my subscription." Include your practice name and the email address used at signup.

You can also cancel directly through your Paddle.com customer portal — the link is in any billing email we've sent you.

We don't require a phone call or a reason. If you want to cancel, just tell us and we'll take care of it.

2. What happens when you cancel

Here's exactly what happens, step by step:

Immediately after cancellation

  • Your subscription stops at the end of the current billing month
  • Your voice receptionist continues working normally until that date — no interruption
  • We'll send you a confirmation email within one business day
  • You keep access to all features until the end of the billing period

After the billing period ends

  • Your dedicated phone number is released from our system
  • The phone number may be reassigned to another practice — if you reactivate, you'll get a new number
  • Your call history and booking records are deleted within 30 days
  • All setup configurations associated with your practice are removed

3. Billing and refunds

No partial refunds

Because Annsa is a month-to-month software service, no partial refunds are provided, but users can cancel anytime to prevent future billing. If you cancel mid-month, you keep access for the rest of that billing period.

For example: if you cancel on the 15th of a 30-day month, you'll have access until the end of that month. We won't charge you for the following month.

No credits for unused time

We don't issue credits for unused service days. If you didn't use the service for a period — for example, if you were between practice locations or had a slow month — we don't offset that against future bills.

Payment failure and cancellation

If your payment fails and isn't updated within 14 days, your subscription will be marked as at-risk. If it remains unresolved after 30 days, the account is automatically cancelled. In this case, no refund is issued — the service was offered during the period.

What to do if you have a billing dispute

If you believe you've been charged incorrectly, email ammarthemediamanager@gmail.com within 7 days of the charge. Include your practice name, the charge date, and a brief description of the issue. We'll review and respond within one business day.

4. Data deletion after cancellation

When your subscription ends, the following data is deleted within 30 days:

  • Your practice name, contact details, and account information
  • Call history and booking records
  • Your knowledge file and setup configuration
  • Your dedicated phone number (released from our system)
  • Any email communications stored in our system

We retain financial records (subscription start date, billing history, payment amounts) for the period required by financial regulations. This data is retained separately and is not associated with your practice's operational data.

You can request immediate deletion of your data at any time before the 30-day period by emailing ammarthemediamanager@gmail.com.

5. Reactivating your account

If you cancel and later decide to come back, you can reactivate by contacting us at ammarthemediamanager@gmail.com.

When you reactivate:

  • You'll be set up as a new client — your previous configuration may not be available
  • Your phone number will be a new number (we can't reclaim the previous one)
  • Setup takes 48 hours from payment, same as a new client
  • Pricing remains $497/month — no special reactivation pricing

6. Exit survey

When you cancel, we'll send you a short email survey with two questions:

  1. What's the main reason you're leaving?
  2. Is there anything we could have done differently?

This takes less than two minutes to complete. Your answers help us improve the service for practices that stay.

If your stated reason is something we can address — for example, a feature gap or a setup issue — our team may follow up with a single email offering to help. This is optional for you to respond to.

If you mention a competitor or express a specific dissatisfaction, our founder may send one personal reply. We do not run retention email sequences designed to wear you down.

7. Questions about cancellation

If you have any questions before cancelling, we're happy to help:

  • Email: ammarthemediamanager@gmail.com
  • Response time: Within one business day during US business hours

Common questions before cancelling:

"Can I pause my subscription instead?"

Yes. If you're between locations, taking a break, or just need a short pause, we can suspend your service for up to 30 days. During this period, your configuration is saved and your phone number is held. Email us to request a pause.

"What if I'm not seeing results?"

Before cancelling, email us — we can review your setup, adjust your knowledge file, or address specific concerns. Many issues that look like "the service isn't working" are actually setup issues we can fix quickly.

"Can I get a refund for this month?"

No. We don't offer refunds for partial months, as explained in section 3. If you have a specific billing dispute, email us within 7 days of the charge and we'll review it.
